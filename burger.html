Recipe for making a burger

Ingredients

For Aalo Patties

  1. 2 potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  2. ¼ cup peas / matar (boiled)
  3. ¼ tsp turmeric / haldi
  4. 1 cup bread crumbs
  5. ½ tsp kashmiri chilli powder
  6. ½ tsp coriander powder
  7. oil for deep frying
  8. ¼ tsp cumin powder / jeera powder
  9. ¼ tsp pepper (crushed)
  10. salt to taste
  11. ½ tsp ginger garlic paste
  12. ¼ cup thick poha / beaten rice / aval (washed)

For Maida Paste

  1. 3 tbsp maida / plain flour / all purpose flour
  2. 2 tbsp corn flour
  3. ¼ tsp pepper (crushed)
  4. ¼ tsp salt
  5. ¼ cup water

Other Ingredients

  1. 3 tbsp eggless mayonnaise
  2. 3 tbsp tomato sauce
  3. 1 tsp chilli sauce
  4. 4 burger bun
  5. few lettuce leaves
  6. 1 to mato (slice)
  7. 1 onion (rings)

Instructions

  • firstly, take 2 boiled and mashed potatoes, ¼ cup boiled peas and spices.
  • combine everything and prepare patties.
  • dip into maida paste and roll in bread crumbs.
  • deep fry in hot oil or bake at 180 degree celsius for 20 minutes or till it turns golden and crisp.
  • cut half the burger bun and spread a tsp of prepared burger sauce on both sides.
  • on bottom side of bun place few lettuce followed by prepared aloo patties.
  • again spread a tsp of burger sauce.
  • place 2 slice of tomato and 2 rings of onion.
  • cover with burger bun and press slightly.
  • finally, serve aloo tikki burger with french fries or potato chips.
